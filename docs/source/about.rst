About this package
==================

``wavelet_noise`` is a package for separating the coherent and incoherent parts of a signal and analyze the statistical properties of these two components. In a conference paper, we argued that these can be interpreted as the hydrodynamic and acoustic/near-wall turbulence constituents of the pressure fluctuations at the wall. This interpretation was originally proposed in a paper by :ref:`Kang et al. [1] <[kang2023]>`, with some methodological issues that we hope to address.
